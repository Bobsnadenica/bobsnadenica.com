Planning

What does MyCompany build?
MyCompany builds websites, landing pages, client portals, iOS and Android apps, SEO-ready web platforms, cloud architecture, monitoring layers, integrations, and delivery roadmaps backed by business analysis.
Can you help before the build starts?
Yes. Business analysis, requirement framing, user flows, service mapping, and technical direction are all part of the offer. If the scope is still unclear, that is often the right place to begin.
Do you only take full projects?
No. MyCompany can support one focused need such as SEO cleanup, cloud review, monitoring setup, technical rescue, or a senior assessment before a larger delivery starts.

Delivery

Can one project combine multiple services?
Yes. Many projects need more than one track, such as website build plus SEO, mobile app plus API delivery, or business analysis plus cloud architecture and monitoring.
How do projects usually start?
The cleanest start is the project form. It captures business context, services needed, timeline, budget range, audience, and delivery notes, then opens a structured email draft.
Who do we communicate with?
You communicate directly with a senior engineer. The goal is short feedback loops, clear technical decisions, and less noise between problem and solution.

Growth and product

Do you provide SEO as a real service, not just basic keywords?
Yes. The focus is technical SEO, site structure, performance, content architecture, schema readiness, crawlability, and the build decisions that help search visibility after launch.
Can you build both iOS and Android apps?
Yes. MyCompany delivers iOS and Android projects, along with API connectivity, release preparation, monitoring, and the product logic around the app itself.
Do you work on internal systems as well as client-facing products?
Yes. Internal tools, operations systems, reporting flows, CRM-connected portals, and automation layers are all in scope.

Operations and support

Do you provide monitoring and support after launch?
Yes. MyCompany can stay involved with observability, uptime review, incident visibility, release support, and ongoing technical improvements.
Can you help rescue an existing project?
Yes. Rescue work can include architecture review, SEO cleanup, product triage, cloud re-framing, debugging, performance work, or stabilizing a project that has lost direction.
Do you work only locally or internationally?
MyCompany supports English and Bulgarian communication and can work with clients internationally as long as the project goals, process, and ownership are clear.

Still have a project-specific question?

Use the guided contact form and describe the situation. That usually gets to a useful answer faster than a generic intro call.
