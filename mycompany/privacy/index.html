Who we are

MyCompany is an IT services business offering website delivery, mobile app development, SEO, cloud architecture, monitoring, business analysis, and senior technical execution.

What we collect

If you use the project form, you may choose to provide your name, company, email address, website, project goals, timeline, budget range, and technical notes. This information is used only to prepare your email draft and to understand your inquiry once you send it.

  • The site does not store the project form content in a site database.
  • The site does not require an account or login.
  • The site does not process payments.

How the project form works

The form on this website is a client-side helper. When you submit it, your own email application opens with a prefilled draft addressed to hello@mycompany.com. You stay in control of whether to send that email.

  • The form itself does not transmit your message to MyCompany until you send the email from your mail app.
  • If you email MyCompany directly, the message is handled through the email provider you use.

Hosting and logs

This website is hosted as a static site on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosting platforms, GitHub may process standard technical request information such as IP address, browser details, or request logs for security, performance, and platform operations.

MyCompany does not add extra tracking cookies or custom analytics in this version of the site. If analytics or third-party tools are added later, this policy should be updated.

Your choices

You can choose not to use the form and instead contact MyCompany directly by email. If you want information removed from an email conversation, use the contact address and request deletion or correction.

  • You decide whether to send the email generated by the form.
  • You can avoid sharing optional fields if they are not relevant.
  • You can request updates or removal of project inquiry information shared by email.

Contact

Questions about privacy or data handling can be sent to hello@mycompany.com.

Need to send a project brief instead?

The fastest route is still the guided contact form. It helps structure the request without adding a backend form service.
