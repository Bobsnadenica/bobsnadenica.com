Fast Rules For SREs

Simple habits that prevent noisy incidents from turning into bigger outages.

Verify scope first

Check the active account, region, cluster, namespace, host, and inventory before touching anything. Half of incident pain comes from fixing the wrong place.

Read before write

Look at logs, events, describe output, plans, and health checks before restarts or applies. Evidence disappears quickly after you change state.

Change one variable

Restart one deployment, target one host, or apply one reviewed plan. Small blast radius makes rollback and root cause much easier.

Capture the clue trail

Write down timestamps, pod names, instance IDs, request IDs, and exact commands. That turns troubleshooting into a repeatable playbook instead of memory work.
