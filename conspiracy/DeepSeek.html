The claim that the Apollo moon landings were staged by NASA. Debunked by multiple evidence sources, yet persists in popular culture.

Critical Thinking Framework

When evaluating conspiracy theories or any extraordinary claims, researchers recommend applying systematic critical thinking:

1. Source Evaluation

Examine the credibility of sources, their expertise, potential biases, and whether they provide verifiable evidence.

2. Evidence Assessment

Look for empirical evidence, consider alternative explanations, and check if claims are falsifiable.

3. Logical Consistency

Analyze whether the theory relies on logical fallacies, special pleading, or ad hoc explanations.
