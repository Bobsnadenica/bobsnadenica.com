Conspiracy Archive

A professional, neutral exploration of the most enduring and controversial alternative theories in modern history.

Historical

Moon Landing Hoax

The theory that NASA faked the Apollo 11 moon landing in 1969 on a film set. Claims include inconsistent shadows, missing stars, and a waving flag in vacuum.

Assassination

JFK Assassination Conspiracy

Alternative theories surrounding the 1963 assassination of President John F. Kennedy, including alleged involvement of the CIA, Mafia, or multiple shooters.

Global

9/11 Inside Job

Claims that the September 11 attacks were either allowed to happen or orchestrated by elements within the U.S. government as a false flag operation.

Society

The Illuminati & New World Order

The belief in a secretive elite organization manipulating world events toward establishing a totalitarian global government.

Science

Flat Earth Theory

The modern revival of the idea that Earth is a flat disc, often centered on the North Pole, with Antarctica forming an ice wall around the edge.

Health

Chemtrails & Geoengineering

The claim that contrails from aircraft are actually chemical or biological agents deliberately sprayed for weather modification or population control.
