Wild Conspiracy Theories

Moon Landing Hoax

The moon landing was faked and footage was filmed in a Hollywood studio.

Proponents of this theory claim that the American flag appears to be waving in the wind despite the lack of atmosphere on the moon. They also point out that there are no stars visible in the pictures taken by the astronauts.

New World Order

A secretive group of powerful individuals and organizations are working to create a one-world government.

Believers in this theory point to supposed evidence such as the increased use of surveillance technology and the rise of globalization.

Illuminati

A secret society founded in the 18th century is still active today and exerting control over world events.

Some believe that the Illuminati is responsible for major historical events such as the French Revolution and the assassination of JFK.

Flat Earth

The Earth is flat, and governments and other organizations are covering up this fact.

Flat earthers claim that the horizon always appears to be at eye level and that the lack of visible curvature of the Earth is evidence of its flatness.

9/11 Truth

The 9/11 attacks were not the result of terrorism, but rather a false flag operation orchestrated by the US government.

Some believe that the collapse of the Twin Towers was caused by controlled demolitions, and that the US government had prior knowledge of the attacks.
