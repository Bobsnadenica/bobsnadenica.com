HOW IT WORKS

The map clears as you move

Your location and movement reveal the fog tile by tile. The more you walk, the more of the real world becomes visible on your map.

01

Open the map

Start with a fog-covered world map and choose whether to explore on your own or as part of a shared discovery journey.

02

Walk in real life

Your movement reveals the surrounding map. Streets, parks, neighborhoods, and entire regions become visible as you physically explore them.

03

Track progress

Count steps, monitor revealed territory, and keep pushing toward complete discovery of your city, country, or even the whole world.
